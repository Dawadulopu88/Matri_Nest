Our story

The safe nest every mom and baby deserve.

মাতৃNest started from a simple frustration: maternal and child care in Bangladesh is scattered across phone numbers, pharmacies and word-of-mouth — and finding it shouldn't cost a mother her peace of mind.

MamaNest

Support through pregnancy and postpartum — doctor appointments, medicine delivery, health tracking, mental wellness and emergency response, all under one roof.

BabyNest

Care that continues after birth — growth tracking, vaccination schedules, pediatric appointments and nursing support for the first years of your child's life.

Verified care

Every doctor and nurse on মাতৃNest is screened before they reach you.

One nest, every need

Appointments, medicine and records live in one place — no more juggling apps.

Built around you
