Support

How can we help?

Answers to the things mothers ask us most. Can't find yours — reach out and we'll get back within a day.

How do I book a doctor's appointment?

Go to Appointments, choose a specialization, and pick a date and time that works for you. You'll see your past bookings on the same page.

Can I track my health data over time?

Yes — the Health page lets you log weight, blood pressure, glucose and symptoms, and keeps your last 10 entries visible at a glance.

What do I do in an emergency?

Use the Emergency page for an SOS alert, or Ambulance to request fast dispatch. Both are reachable from your dashboard at any time.

Is my information private?

Your health records and appointments are tied only to your account and are never shown to other users.
